About Us
Circle K International is the largest student-led collegiate service organization in the world. While encompassing all three tenets of Service, Fellowship, and Leadership - we are student leaders that work together to serve the community while making memorable bonds. We support the Pediatric Trauma Program, Feeding America, and the Kiwanis Family House through various fundraisers that allow us to donate to these charities and help at a larger scale.
