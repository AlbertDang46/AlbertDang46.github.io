Member Recognition Program
The Membership Recognition Program (MRP) is tier-based system that we have here at Circle K where members can be recognized for their dedication and service to the club. Recipients of MRP are those who exemplify themselves in our three tenets – service, leadership, and fellowship!

The MRP term repeats annually, starting from Spring quarter and ending at the Winter quarter. Here at Circle K at UCR, we have a tradition of celebrating the achievements of our MRP recipients during Spring quarter, following the end of each MRP term! Every year, the MRP requirements changes slightly, but remain similar for the most part. You’ll be able to find the MRP requirements for the 2018-19 term here!

​ Contact our Member Recognition Chair, Gus Del Castillo for more information!
Master Records Sheet
The Master Records Sheet (MRS) is a document where members can look up which the amount of hours spent at Circle K events and events they have attended in the last year. The MRS also has information on each member's Member Recognition Program (MRP) status. For more information on the MRS or your MRP status, contact our secretary, Amy Kobayashi.​
