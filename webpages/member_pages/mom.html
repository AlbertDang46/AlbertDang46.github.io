Members of the Month (MOM)
Here is a cohesive list of all the Members of the Months for the 2018-2019 term! It is ordered from newest to oldest, top to bottom respectively. All commentary is made by last year's Member Recognition Chair, Andrew Chang. Make sure you check out the club's calendar and attend all the events you can! Be an altruistic and active member and you might be on here too!

Once the year starts rolling, you might be able to appear on here too for the 2019-2020 term!
Winter
Jack Chen has been an active member during December and January while coming out to events with a big smile :) He's gone to events such as Jingle Bell run, soup kitchen, Girl Scout Megadrop, and so much more! He's a great person to talk to and is a model for what every Circle K member should strive to become.
November
At our last general meeting, we recognized our new November Member of the Month, Alfonso Navarrete! He has been a great member and is always willing to lend a hand. He's gone out to events such as Fall Install, R'Pantry, socials, and so much more! Overall, he demonstrates what it means to be a Circle K member and we are grateful for his commitment to this club.
October
Congratulations Gustavo Del Castillo on becoming our October MOM! This friendly guy has been to October WAW, committee meetings, workdays, and so much more! He has an overall positive outlook of this club and is someone who follows our 3 tenets of Circle K, Service, Leadership, and Fellowship.
May
Congratulations to Samantha Del Castillo for being our May Member of the Month! She has been a friendly face and went out to events such as Kevin’s PYOS, MRP Palooza, Maddie’s cheer workshop, family social, Key 2 College, committee meetings, Lei workdays, KRCB cleanup, Banquet, MoM Dinner, and service insomnia! She has been a real contributor to our family and we are all grateful for her dedication to the club
April
Congratulations to June Park for being our April Member of the Month! She has been putting herself out there lately by going to events such as Welcome Week, Arbor Day, April WaW, committee meetings, board meetings, and workdays! - if you ever see her, be sure to say hi and congratulate her for getting MOM!
