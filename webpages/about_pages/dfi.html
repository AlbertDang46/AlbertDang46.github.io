District Fundraising Initiative (DFI)
DFI stands for District Fundraising Initiative and DFIs are the three causes that Circle K supports through fundraising! This year's DFIs are Trevor Project, Kiwanis Family House, and Pediatric Trauma Program.
Trevor Project
The Trevor Project is the leading national organization providing crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer & questioning youth.
Kiwanis Family House
The Kiwanis Family House was created to provide temporary, restful, secure, and low-cost housing to families of seriously ill patients that are being treated at the University of California Davis Medical Center in Sacramento, CA. The funds raised will go towards further supporting these families.
Pediatric Trauma Program
Pediatric Trauma Program, or PTP, works toward preventing unintentional injury in children. Unintentional injury is the #1 leading cause of death in kids ages 14 and under, and 90% of these injuries can actually be prevented. This program utilizes their knowledge and funds to train pediatric doctors and nurses, purchase essential medical equipment, and create or expand community outreach programs. The funds raised will go towards aiding this cause, as well as providing safety items for children.
